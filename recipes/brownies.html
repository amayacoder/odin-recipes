Vegan brownies

Here is a recipe for my tasty vegan brownies!

They are chocolatey and delicious. Eat them on their own or with cream or icecream!

They seem to get even better as they age!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 200 g dark chcocolate
  • 1/2 tsp coffee granules
  • 80 g vegan butter
  • 125 g self-raising flour
  • 70 g ground almonds
  • 50 g cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 250 g golden caster sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 170C/150C fan/gas 3½. Grease and line a 20cm square tin with baking parchment.
  2. Combine the flaxseed with 6 tbsp water and set aside for at least 5 mins.
  3. In a saucepan, melt 120g chocolate, the coffee and margarine with 60ml water on a low heat. Allow to cool slightly.
  4. Put the flour, almonds, cocoa, baking powder and ¼ tsp salt in a bowl and stir to remove any lumps.
  5. Using a hand whisk, mix the sugar into the melted chocolate mixture, and beat well until smooth and glossy, ensuring all the sugar is well dissolved.
  6. Stir in the flaxseed mixture, vanilla extract and remaining chocolate, then the flour mixture.
  7. Spoon into the prepared tin.
  8. Bake for 35-45 mins until a skewer inserted in the middle comes out clean with moist crumbs. Allow to cool in the tin completely, then cut into squares.
