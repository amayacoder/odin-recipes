Baked Oats

Here is a recipe for my tasty vegan baked oats!

They can be cooked in the airfryer and go very well with yoghurt. Why not try adding a different fruit or even chcocolate chips?

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 scoop vegan protein
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed + 3 tbsp water
  • 3/4 cup soya milk
  • 1 tbsp almond butter (optional)
  • Handful of frozen raspberries

Instructions

  1. Combine flaxseed and water to make flax egg. Set aside.
  2. Blend all ingredients, except raspberries until relatively smooth.
  3. Pour into silicon dish and sprinkle the raspberries on top.
  4. Bake for 18 minutes in the airdryer at 175 Â°C.
