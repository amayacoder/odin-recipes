Scrambled Tofu

Here is a recipe for my tasty scrambled tofu!

It can be enjoyed on some toast, maybe with some avocado or houmous.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp Butter
  • 1 Block Tofu
  • 1 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Chilli flakes
  • 1 tbsp Soya milk

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in pan.
  2. Once butter is melted, break tofu with hands into pan.
  3. Cook for a couple minutes, then add turmeric, chilli flakes and soy sauce.
  4. Cook for a couple more minutes. Stir in soya milk and turn off the heat.
